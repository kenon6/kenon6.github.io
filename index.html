    I truly loved my late husband. He gave me a lot to be thankful for. He was kind when he wanted to be, funny most of the time and he was rarely angry. Despite that, he never put my needs over his. I helped him get through life, putting my own dreams on the bench and he decided to see another woman. Now I'm not like Medea. Although I felt like killing him, I didn't. I also didn't have the guts to leave my son with him like Nora did. So I stuck with him and put up with it till he died. Why did I feel the need to put myself second in the relationship? I have no idea. I believe with Fictional Feminists, we can prevent women from feeling that way. We are here to empower!
